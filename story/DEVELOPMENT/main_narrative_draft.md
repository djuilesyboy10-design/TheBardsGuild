"The others hear the music and fear the dance, Lore Keeper. But I? I see the ink. My records speak of the Mephala cults and the Dark Brotherhood, yet this new 'vibration' feels different—it is clinical. It is as if we are being pinned to a board for study. I do not like the stillness I see in the pupils of the city’s beggars and servants lately; it is a stillness that belongs to a biography being finished by an invisible hand."


"I am a son of the desert, born of survivors who learned that the only way to endure a storm is to become the stone. Ally worries for the locks, but I worry for the record. If history is being unwritten, then someone must be here to write it back into existence."

The Instrument: The Poet’s Quill
He sets down his tool and offers you a quill. It is heavy, carved from a fossilized bone that feels cold even in the warmth of the archive.

"Take the Poet’s Quill. It is the recorder’s spine. While Jo'Zhaf finds the path and Jack finds the lie, this quill will anchor the truth. As you find these 'future records,' use this to transcribe them. It will not allow the ink to move. It forces the 'unsung song' to stay on the page where it belongs. It is my discipline, given to you."

Shane’s Stoic Insight
"Do not let the 'stillness' infect you, ^PCName. I have seen the same face in three different cities this month, always watching, always still. They think we are insects. But they forget that even a pinned insect has stings. My family survived the wastes by remembering what others forgot. We will do the same for the guild."


"Go to Seyda Neen. If this alchemist, Xeech, is to be a powerful ally as Ally believes, we need her recorded in our books before the Piper turns her into a ghost. Use the quill. Do not let the history stay unwritten."

The Lore Keeper’s Completed Symphony
With Shane’s Quill, you now have the final piece of the mechanical loop:

Jo'Zhaf’s Drum: Locates the general area of the "Fracture."

Jack’s Lute: Pinpoints the specific "Stolen" object.

Daisy’s Flute: Stabilizes the object so it can be handled.

Shane’s Quill: Permanently records the object into the Guild's Archive, "locking" that piece of the future so it cannot be altered.
